1. Executive Summary
VetCare Sri Lanka is a comprehensive digital platform designed to revolutionize veterinary healthcare services across Sri Lanka. The platform connects pet owners with licensed veterinarians through both in-person appointments and remote telemedicine consultations, making quality veterinary care accessible to all Sri Lankans regardless of their geographic location.
The platform addresses critical gaps in the current veterinary healthcare ecosystem including limited access to qualified veterinarians in rural areas, lack of digital booking systems, absence of telemedicine options, and fragmented pet health record management. By leveraging modern technology, VetCare Sri Lanka aims to become the leading veterinary services platform in the country.
1.1 Key Highlights
•	Multi-platform solution: Web application, mobile apps (iOS & Android), and admin dashboard
•	Telemedicine capabilities: Video, audio, and chat consultations with file sharing
•	Comprehensive booking system: In-clinic appointments, home visits, and emergency services
•	Integrated payment solutions: Support for local payment gateways including PayHere and Dialog eZ Cash
•	Multi-language support: Sinhala, Tamil, and English interfaces
•	AI-powered features: Symptom analysis, smart triage, and predictive health alerts
•	IoT integration: Smart collar and wearable device connectivity
1.2 Target Market
Sri Lanka has an estimated pet population of over 3 million dogs alone, with a growing culture of pet ownership in urban and suburban areas. The target market includes urban pet owners seeking convenience, rural pet owners with limited access to veterinary services, commercial pet businesses, animal shelters, and veterinary professionals looking to expand their reach.
1.3 Business Model
VetCare Sri Lanka operates on a multi-revenue model including commission-based fees (10-20% per booking), subscription plans for veterinarians and clinics, premium listing fees, telemedicine platform fees, and integrated e-commerce for pet products and medications.

3. Problem Statement & Solution
3.1 Current Challenges in Sri Lankan Veterinary Care
3.1.1 Limited Geographic Access
Qualified veterinarians are predominantly concentrated in urban centers such as Colombo, Kandy, and Galle. Pet owners in rural areas often travel hours to access basic veterinary services, leading to delayed treatment and poor health outcomes for animals.
3.1.2 Absence of Digital Infrastructure
Most veterinary clinics in Sri Lanka operate without digital booking systems. Pet owners must physically visit clinics or make phone calls to schedule appointments, leading to long wait times and scheduling inefficiencies.
3.1.3 Fragmented Health Records
Pet health records are typically maintained on paper, making it difficult to track vaccination schedules, treatment history, and ongoing health conditions. When pets visit different veterinarians, their complete medical history is often unavailable.
3.1.4 No Emergency Response System
There is no centralized system for pet emergencies. Pet owners struggle to find available veterinarians during off-hours or identify the nearest emergency veterinary facility.
3.1.5 Limited Telemedicine Options
Unlike human healthcare, veterinary telemedicine is virtually non-existent in Sri Lanka. Many minor consultations that could be handled remotely require in-person visits, wasting time for both pet owners and veterinarians.
3.2 VetCare Sri Lanka Solution
VetCare Sri Lanka addresses each of these challenges through a comprehensive digital platform:
Challenge	VetCare Solution
Limited Geographic Access	Telemedicine consultations allow pet owners to connect with veterinarians regardless of location. Home visit booking brings veterinary care to doorsteps.
No Digital Infrastructure	Online booking system with real-time availability, automated reminders, and queue management reduces wait times and improves efficiency.
Fragmented Health Records	Centralized digital health records accessible by authorized veterinarians ensure complete medical history is always available.
No Emergency System	Emergency SOS feature instantly connects pet owners with available emergency vets. GPS-based nearest clinic finder provides directions.
No Telemedicine	Full-featured telemedicine module with video, audio, chat, and file sharing capabilities enables remote consultations.

5.2 Appointment Booking System
The booking system is the core transaction engine of VetCare Sri Lanka, handling all appointment scheduling, modifications, and cancellations.
5.2.1 Booking Types
Type	Description
In-Clinic Visit	Standard appointment at the veterinarian's clinic. Pet owner travels to the clinic location. Suitable for physical examinations, procedures, and treatments requiring equipment.
Home Visit	Veterinarian travels to the pet owner's location. Ideal for multiple pets, elderly pet owners, large animals, or pets that experience travel anxiety. Additional fees may apply.
Telemedicine	Remote consultation via video, audio, or chat. Suitable for follow-ups, minor concerns, medication questions, and initial assessments. Most convenient and often most affordable option.
Emergency	Priority booking for urgent cases. Connects with available emergency veterinarians. May include higher fees for after-hours or immediate availability.
5.2.2 Booking Flow
Step 1: Service Selection - Pet owner selects consultation type and service category
Step 2: Veterinarian Selection - Browse and select preferred veterinarian
Step 3: Date & Time Selection - Choose from available time slots
Step 4: Pet Selection - Select which pet(s) the appointment is for
Step 5: Symptom Description - Describe the reason for visit and any symptoms
Step 6: Payment - Complete payment through preferred payment method
Step 7: Confirmation - Receive booking confirmation via app and SMS
5.2.3 Booking Management

10. Database Design
The database design follows a relational model with PostgreSQL as the primary database. This section outlines the main entities and their relationships.
10.1 Core Entities
10.1.1 Users
Stores all user accounts including pet owners, veterinarians, and administrators.
•	Fields: id, email, phone, password_hash, user_type, status, created_at, updated_at
•	Relationships: Has many pets (pet owners), has one vet profile (veterinarians)
10.1.2 Pet Owners
Extended profile information for pet owner users.
•	Fields: id, user_id, first_name, last_name, profile_image, language_preference
•	Relationships: Has many pets, has many addresses, has many appointments
10.1.3 Veterinarians
Professional profile for veterinarian users.
•	Fields: id, user_id, license_number, specializations, qualifications, bio, consultation_fee
•	Relationships: Belongs to clinics, has many appointments, has many reviews
10.1.4 Clinics
Veterinary clinic information.
•	Fields: id, name, address, phone, email, operating_hours, facilities, status
•	Relationships: Has many veterinarians, has many appointments
10.1.5 Pets
Pet profiles with health information.
•	Fields: id, owner_id, name, species, breed, date_of_birth, gender, weight, microchip_id
•	Relationships: Belongs to owner, has many health records, has many appointments
10.1.6 Appointments
Booking records for all consultation types.
•	Fields: id, pet_id, vet_id, clinic_id, type, status, scheduled_at, duration, notes
•	Relationships: Belongs to pet, veterinarian, clinic; has one consultation, has one payment
10.1.7 Consultations
Detailed consultation session data.
•	Fields: id, appointment_id, start_time, end_time, notes, diagnosis, recording_url
•	Relationships: Belongs to appointment, has many prescriptions, has many files
10.1.8 Prescriptions
Digital prescription records.
•	Fields: id, consultation_id, medications (JSON), instructions, valid_until, signature
•	Relationships: Belongs to consultation
10.1.9 Health Records
Pet medical history entries.
•	Fields: id, pet_id, record_type, title, description, date, attachments, vet_id
•	Relationships: Belongs to pet, may belong to veterinarian
10.1.10 Payments
Transaction records.
•	Fields: id, appointment_id, user_id, amount, currency, method, status, transaction_id
•	Relationships: Belongs to appointment, belongs to user
10.1.11 Reviews
User reviews for veterinarians.
•	Fields: id, vet_id, user_id, appointment_id, rating, comment, photos, response
•	Relationships: Belongs to veterinarian, belongs to user, belongs to appointment
10.2 Supporting Entities
•	Addresses: User address management for home visits
•	Availability: Veterinarian schedule and time slots
•	Notifications: Notification logs and delivery status
•	Messages: Chat messages during consultations
•	Files: Uploaded documents and media
•	Vaccinations: Vaccination records with schedules
•	Wallet: User wallet balance and transactions
•	Promocodes: Discount codes and promotions
